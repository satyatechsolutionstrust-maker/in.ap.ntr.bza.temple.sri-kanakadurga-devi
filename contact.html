About the Founder

VRDS Prasad is a devoted spiritual leader and the visionary founder of Sri Kanaka Durga Devi Temple. With a deep-rooted passion for preserving Hindu Dharma and serving the community, he established this sacred temple to provide a spiritual haven for devotees in Vijayawada and beyond.

Under his guidance, the temple has grown into a vibrant center of worship, education, and charitable service.

"సేవే పరమో ధర్మః — Service is the highest Dharma. Through devotion to Maa Durga, we find strength, peace, and purpose."
