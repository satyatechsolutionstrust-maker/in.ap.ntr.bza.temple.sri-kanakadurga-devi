Our History

Sri Kanaka Durga Devi Temple is dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga and the propagation of Hindu Dharma. Located in Ramarajya Nagar, Vijayawada, our temple serves as a spiritual center for devotees seeking divine blessings, peace, and prosperity.

We conduct daily poojas, special archanas, and grand festivals throughout the year, bringing the community together in devotion and celebration.

Founded over 25 years ago, the temple has grown into a vibrant spiritual hub, attracting thousands of devotees from across the region.
